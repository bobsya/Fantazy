history

In Lucis, Noctis and his friends Gladiolus, Ignis and Prompto are en route to Accordo's capital city of Altissia, where Noctis's wedding to Lunafreya is to take place. Along the way, they receive news of the empire's attack on Insomnia and theft of the Crystal, and that Noctis's father King Regis has been assassi-nated. They meet up with Crownsguard commander Cor Leonis, who tasks Noctis with retrieving the weapons of the Lucii—the ancient kings of Lucis—in order to rescue the Crystal and reclaim his throne.

The party also comes across Niflheim's chancellor Ardyn Izunia, who guides them to the Astral Titan at the Disc of Cauthess. After he fulfills Titan's trial and fights off imperial forces attacking the Astral, Noctis learns that Lu-nafreya persuaded Titan to lend him his power.
